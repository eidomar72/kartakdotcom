Chilling With Winnie The Pooh February 6, 2023 | 11:30 AM EST My friend Winnie the Pooh and I sat back and watched as the market pushed high after open. We saw and heard the cheers of perma-bulls buying into the rally. FOMO-ing and YOLO-ing left and right as they sweat down a storm. With every pull the market gave us, their heart skipped a beat. We sat there and said to ourselves, what a bunch of degenerates.

Two clear facts we have on our hands; reality is that the market is a piece of shit and doesn’t deserve the pump it’s getting. Meaning the pump is nothing more then a mixture of perma-bears forced to cover, hedge funds loading new short positions and using retail to liquidate whatever long position they have. Second fact is that retail is being too greedy and will eventually lose the battle, that is whether they’re short or long.

Anyway, long story short, the market gave us a clear double top in the morning for a short play. Followed by a beautiful hammer candle right at the ES 4110 and SPY 408.5 supports to go long. Being patient was key and getting into the market with a clear mind of no bias would have awarded you nicely.

All you need to do is to learn to crawl through the aisle at a grocery store while not hungry before walking.
