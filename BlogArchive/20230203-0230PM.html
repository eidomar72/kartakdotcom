Somebody Call Newton, Apple Is Defying Gravity! February 3, 2023 | 02:30 PM EST Apple, you’re one hell of a beast!

There’s nothing stopping that rally. For once, the market is making me feel like I know what I’m doing. I profited handsomely from the morning rally. switched short on Amazon out of boredom (but mainly because we’re way too overextended after bad earnings). That worked out well. Maybe the market makers are out on Friday and not watching my screen—that must be it. There’s no way this is happening out of pure talent and good market reading. Maybe this is the turnaround moment for me to switch from a bad trader to a less bad trader. Is this what puberty feels like? My voice sounds a bit richer. Why am I walking with a confident stride?

Or is it just greed?

It seems like the market just woke up from a comma. It realized that bad earnings don’t mean dip buying, geopolitical tension between major countries is actually a scary thing, high employment numbers (low unemployment numbers) are actually bad for inflation, and pausing rate hikes might not have been the best move. I guess FOMO-ers, YOLO-ers, and dip buyers need to learn somehow. Liquidation doesn’t come from thin air; someone needs to lose money for market makers to make some. Somebody needs to flip the burger! Anyway, is AI still a thing or are we over that cycle yet, asking for short-seller friend.
