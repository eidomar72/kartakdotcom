Blog Post - Jan 25, 2023
Guidance Who? Nothing Stoping The Rally
Know why the tickers gapped. Pull out your favorite handy-dandy News Website. Everyone needs to have a news website that they can trust. Mine are Thefly.com, Stocktitan.net and Finviz.com to look for press-release headlines and articles. Start going through reasoning why your watchlist stocks gapped. Understand whether the headline is legit or just bullshit. If it’s legit, then start planning your play in favor of the headline with supply and demand zones, support and resistance lines and how the stock last trader when it had volume (when it last gapped). Most of the time it repeats its previous action. If the headline seems bullshit or if the stock is gapping on no news, there’s probably is an underlying reasoning…do some more digging and know what stock is susceptible to previous pumps and dumps (here I check simply check Twitter to connect dots).

I like looking through any overnight Bankruptcy and Private Placement news and SEC filings. Each of these have their own style of trade. I think every trader needs to know how to trade bankruptcy and private placement offerings. If you don’t want to, then go grab coffee. Take a small mental break and prep talk yourself

Not so much of a step but this is a good time where I go through my main trading rules; cutting losses, no revenge trading, A+ setups only, trades are taken only when I have a plan, it’s okay to end the day with zero trades, etc...whatever rules that keep your trading consistent. You know the drill…
