The Wolf Wins: Introduction to Moving Averages
A Moving Average (MA) is a technical indicators that is used to smooth out the noise within a price chart by creating a constant updated average price over a set time frame. Moving average is a basic yet extremely important indicator for both traders and investors. Anyone living under rock for the past 50 years can simply identify market conditions in minutes by simply looking at the S&P 500 50-Day MA, 100-Day MA and 200-Day MA.
